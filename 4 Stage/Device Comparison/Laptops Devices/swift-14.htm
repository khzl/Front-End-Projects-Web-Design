Swift 14

Swift 14

Shop Now

Introducing Swift 14. A renovation of the iconic Swift 5blending exceptional thermal design housed with a precision-crafted aluminum chassisand poised to deliver incomparable performance for your biggest breakthroughs.

Exquisitely Designed. Masterfully Crafted.

The premium, aerospace-grade aluminum chassis is CNC-machined to an incredible 14.95 mm and 1.2 kg.1 The masterfully crafted design extends to the eco-conscious OceanGlass touchpad and power button fingerprint reader.

Precision CNC Machining

With a tolerance of +/-0.05 mm, the cover edges are precision diamond-cut within a margin of less than the thickness of a single human hair.

Double Anodized Sides

Eye-catching, double anodized sides protect from corrosion and wearwhile producing a beautiful golden hue that commands attention.

The Multitask Master

Experience the superior performance of 13th Gen Intel Core H-Series Processors1at up to 45W and 14 cores1and certified as an Intel Evo design featuring Intel Unison to deliver a no-compromise laptop experience2.

Up to 1.45x Increase in Performance3

Accomplish more with the latest 13th Gen Intel Core Processors,1 to deliver more ultra-efficient productivity and multi-tasking power.

The Ultimate Laptop Experience

Customized to give you superior performance, responsiveness, and battery lifeIntel Evo laptops are engineered to do it all, without giving up anything2.

PC and Phone Integration

Intel Unison seamlessly connects with your choice of phone. Transfer files & photos, send and receive calls & text messages, and receive & manage phone notifications all from your PC.

Experience Engineered Cooling

Experience high-performing thermal efficiency with TwinAir coolingsupporting multiple cooling modesdual copper heat pipes, and an air inlet keyboard expelling up to 10%4 more heat than a standard keyboard.

TwinAir Cooling

Strategically placed fans dissipate heat in a balanced and controlled manner.

Thermal Performance up by 60.7%

With symmetric TwinAir design, experience better thermal performance than a single fan4.

Cool to the Touch

Rest your palms on the keyboard and feel the cool touch of the Swift 14s surface.

The Display

Get hands-on with the 2.5K1 touch display with Antimicrobial Corning Gorilla Glass,5 boasting an impressive screen-to-body ratio and thin bezels all around. And with Acers arsenal of vision care and color tech, the possibilities are endless.

Video calling has never been so good on the 1440p QHD camerawhich uses Acers TNR (Temporal Noise Reduction) technology for high-quality imagery in low-light conditions along with Acer PurifiedVoice with AI Noise Reduction.

The Connectivity

With 2 USB Type C (Thunderbolt 4 / USB 4) ports, HDMI 2.1, and Wi-Fi 6E6, the Swift 14 has all the essential connections to keep your productivity powered on!
